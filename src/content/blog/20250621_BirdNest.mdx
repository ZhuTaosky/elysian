## 21 June, 2025. Night walk near Olympic Park
Post-graduation limbo. One chapter has ended,but I don't feel quite ready for the next. Feeling a bit lost today.
Really Grateful for my friend Teng Li and his girl friend Xiao Liu for treating me  Luosifen buffet near the South Gate of University. It meant a lot that they took the time to include me——it really warmed my heart at that a bit lonely peroid of time.

![BirdNest](/images/blog/20250621_BirdNest/OlympicTower.jpg)

Today I wrapped up the last errand for my supervisor xls. I mentioned that I was just wandering around Beijing, surprisly, he insisted on treating the three of us graduates to a meal before we head off in different life directions. ALthough we've have been looking forward to this, time didn't permit. Sitll, it speaks volume——desibite his quirky temperament, his character and attitude for his students are ultimately solid.


It has been nearly two years since my last visit the Bird's Nest. Walking alone this time, I found a sense of tranquility and composure that crowd could never offer. Looking back the begining of the journery of Master, Gabby was the first lesson to me---unforgetable teacher influenced how I understand the nature of love. In hindsight, there was a purity and beauty to those thoughts that I still cherish. I once fantasized about confessing my feeling to her by the tranquil spring in Olympic Forest Park, painting for her a picture of my deepest craving for the word. But everything was destined to me. In that experience, the deffects of my character were laid bare for all to see.


两年以来，我经历了第一次对爱情的渴望和又一次对事业的追求，从初读研时的迷茫，建立学术理想，到看透本质，真正的学术自由国不敢再去追寻，但我知道，我爱的是解决问题和革新技术，这是我定义的科研啊，北京，北京，再过不久，我又要留开你。第一次来是 18 年高考结束和琨办理三方协议，而 7 年后的大概同一时间，我也来到相似的节点，可是这次我的前路是上海，我是喜欢北京的，喜欢北航的。这三年，在房老师团队的平台，见识到了顶级的科研团队是如何运作的，徐老师亦师亦友地相处，让我的硕士生活一点也不无聊，也有矛盾，也有不甘，但从此，我想我坚定了一点，为事业生活不顾一切的付出，终将无愧无悔，北京市优秀毕业生的荣誉虽然可能含金量不高，但是为我这三年平添了几分勉励和宽慰。一句话就是“为了事业和追求不顾一切地付出，不一定取得举世无双的成绩，但是一定会无愧无悔自己的时间和精力”。

谢谢盖文武，你的浪漫和有趣的灵魂感染了 22 岁的我，25 岁的韬儿会珍藏那份情难抑、和夜不能寐的思念，未来的我会以更加负责的态度对待自己的感情，打磨自己的志趣、爱好和人格，以及学会宽容和接纳；对事业确立下来，为之不懈奋斗！

希望在读博的四年里，找到爱的人，找到爱的事，有最爱的人陪伴做最爱的事，一生矢志不渝，守护爱的人和事。
